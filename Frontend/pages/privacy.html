Introduction

Welcome to NomaStore. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us through our website. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. It also explains your rights with respect to your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information from you when you use our website. Personal information includes your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment information. We collect this information in order to process your orders, communicate with you about your orders and our products, and improve our website and customer service. We may also use your information to send you promotional materials and offers that we think may interest you.

We collect information from you in several ways, including:

We may also collect information from third-party sources, such as social media platforms, in order to improve our marketing efforts.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to process your orders, communicate with you about your orders and our products, and improve our website and customer service. We may also use your information to send you promotional materials and offers that we think may interest you.

We may share your information with third-party service providers, such as payment processors and shipping providers, in order to provide our products and services to you. These third-party service providers may have access to your personal information, but they are required to protect your information in the same way we do.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights and property, prevent fraud, or protect the safety of our users.
