Privacy Policy

At NomaStore, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our website or services.

What information do we collect?

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment information, such as your credit card or PayPal information
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Information about your purchases and preferences
  • Information about your device and browsing activity on our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited

How do we use your information?

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders and inquiries
  • To personalize your shopping experience and recommend products that may interest you
  • To improve our website and services
  • To prevent fraud and monitor for security purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

How do we protect your information?

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we use SSL encryption to secure your payment information, and we regularly monitor our systems for suspicious activity. However, please keep in mind that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Do we share your information?

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as shipping carriers and payment processors. We may also share your information with our affiliates and partners for marketing purposes, but only if you have given us your consent.

We may disclose your personal information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety or that of others.

How can you manage your information?

You can access and update your personal information by logging into your account on our website. You can also contact us to request that we delete your account and personal information.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience on our website and to analyze how our website is used

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies to remember your preferences, optimize your shopping experience, and provide personalized advertising.

We may also use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to collect information about your use of our website. These services may use cookies and other tracking technologies to track your activity on our website and to provide us with analytics reports. We use this information to improve our website and services.

Marketing communications

If you have opted in to receive marketing communications from us, we may use your personal information to send you promotional emails, newsletters, and other marketing materials. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the emails or by contacting us directly.

Children's privacy

Our website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website or by sending you an email notification.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or our use of your personal information, please contact us at privacy@nomastore.com.
